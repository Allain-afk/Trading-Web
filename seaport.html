6 Major

Seaports in Malaysia

  • Seaports

Port Klang

Malaysia's largest and busiest port, handling over 12 million TEUs annually. A key hub for international trade in Southeast Asia.

Port of Tanjung Pelepas

A major transshipment hub in Johor, known for its state-of-the-art facilities and strategic location near the Strait of Malacca.

Port of Johor

Located in Pasir Gudang, this port specializes in bulk cargo and serves as a key gateway for industrial goods in Johor.

Port of Penang

A vital port for northern Malaysia, connecting Penang Island to global trade routes. Known for its container and cruise terminals.

Port of Bintulu

Located in Sarawak, this port is a key export hub for liquefied natural gas (LNG) and supports the region's energy sector.

Port of Kuantan

Strategically located on the east coast, this port facilitates trade with China and supports Malaysia's industrial zones.
