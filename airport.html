Here are the top 10 airports of

Malaysia

  • Airports

Kuala Lumpur International Airport

Malaysia's main international gateway and largest airport. Handles over 60 million passengers annually with two main terminals. Home to Malaysia Airlines and major hub for AirAsia.

Kota Kinabalu International Airport

Second busiest airport in Malaysia, serving as the main gateway to Sabah and Borneo. Features modern terminals handling both domestic and international flights to major Asian destinations.

Penang International Airport

Third busiest airport in Malaysia, connecting the UNESCO heritage city to major Asian destinations. Recently upgraded with enhanced facilities and a capacity of 6.5 million passengers.

Kuching International Airport

The main gateway to Sarawak, featuring modern facilities and handling over 5 million passengers annually. Offers direct connections to major Malaysian cities and international destinations.

Senai International Airport

Located in Johor, serving the southern region of Peninsular Malaysia. Features cargo facilities and handles flights to regional destinations, supporting the Iskandar Malaysia development region.

Langkawi International Airport

Gateway to Malaysia's popular tourist island, handling both domestic and international flights. Modern facilities supporting tourism with duty-free shopping and convenient access to beach resorts.

Miri Airport

Important regional hub in Sarawak serving the oil and gas industry. Handles domestic flights and some international routes, with modern facilities and expanded terminal capacity.

Tawau Airport

Essential gateway to eastern Sabah, serving nearby diving destinations and palm oil industries. Modern terminal handling domestic flights and connections to major Malaysian cities.

Sultan Ismail Petra Airport

Located in Kelantan, serving the east coast region of Peninsular Malaysia. Recently upgraded facilities handling domestic flights and connecting to major Malaysian cities.
